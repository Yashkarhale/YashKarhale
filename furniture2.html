Our Heritage

The Royal Root Philosophy

Founded in 2005 in the woodworking heartland of North Carolina, Royal Root began as a small family workshop dedicated to preserving traditional furniture-making techniques while innovating for contemporary lifestyles. What started as three generations of the Henderson family crafting custom pieces for local clients has grown into a renowned furniture atelier while maintaining our commitment to artisanal quality.

We believe furniture should tell a story—of the materials used, the hands that shaped it, and the homes it graces. Each Royal Root piece begins with carefully selected hardwoods from sustainably managed forests. Our craftspeople, many of whom trained under European master woodworkers, employ time-honored joinery methods like dovetail, mortise-and-tenon, and finger joints—techniques that ensure durability while showcasing the wood's natural beauty.

Unlike mass-produced furniture designed for temporary use, our creations are built to last generations, developing richer character over time. We balance classic proportions with thoughtful modern adaptations, resulting in pieces that feel both timeless and perfectly suited for today's living spaces.

The Art of Craftsmanship

From raw lumber to finished masterpiece, every Royal Root piece undergoes a meticulous 87-step process combining traditional techniques with modern precision.

Material Selection

We source only premium hardwoods—walnut, oak, cherry, and maple—from sustainable forests in North America and Europe. Each board is hand-selected for grain pattern, color consistency, and structural integrity.

Traditional Joinery

Our craftspeople use time-tested techniques like dovetail joints for drawers, mortise-and-tenon for structural frames, and hand-cut inlays. These methods create superior strength and showcase woodworking mastery.

Finishing Process

We apply 7-12 hand-rubbed layers of non-toxic, eco-friendly finish. This penetrating oil treatment protects while allowing the wood to breathe and age beautifully, developing a lustrous patina over decades.

Our Materials Matter

Royal Root's commitment to sustainability goes beyond using legally harvested woods. We participate in reforestation programs, plant three trees for every one used in our furniture, and optimize material usage to minimize waste. All scrap wood becomes kindling for our workshop's heating system or material for small household items. Even our sawdust is donated to local farmers for animal bedding.

We carefully vet all suppliers to ensure compliance with international forestry protection standards and prioritize American hardwoods when possible to reduce transportation emissions. The natural variations in wood grain mean no two Royal Root pieces are exactly alike—each bears the unique fingerprint of nature's artistry.
